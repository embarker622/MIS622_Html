Many moons ago an quiet dog decided to change the way he lived. He was tired of being the quiet one and watching other dogs gather. So he decided to send out invitations to all the local dogs including one very special dog, Freeda. From puppies he and Freeda would play, go for long walks and explore new things. But lately Freeda started showing less interest in these events and more in parties.

So he sat down to write the invitations. He wrote them to all the dogs that he use to play with. He wrote invitations to dogs that he barely new. He sent special invitations to his parents and twelve brothers and sisters. But when it came to Freeda's he made a special attempt at what he wrote.

He wrote, "If you get this invitation please let me know you got it with a sign."
